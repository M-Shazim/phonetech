Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy outlines how PhoneTech collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Order Data: Products purchased, payment details (securely handled), and order history.
  • Website Usage Data: Cookies, IP address, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders and deliver products.
  • To personalize your shopping experience.
  • To send order updates, offers, and newsletters (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • To improve our services and security.

3. Data Protection

We implement standard security measures to protect your data. Payment details are processed through secure third-party gateways and are never stored on our servers.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as necessary to process your order (e.g., courier companies).

5. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us via the Contact page.

6. Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us.
