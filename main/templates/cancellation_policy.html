Order Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change — here’s how cancellation works at Phonetech.

1. Eligibility for Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled within 6 hours of placing the order.
  • Orders that have been processed or shipped cannot be cancelled.

2. How to Cancel an Order

  • Email us at Phonetech.click@gmail.com with your Order ID.
  • Or call our support team at +92 310 6958010 during working hours (Mon–Sat, 10 AM–6 PM).

3. Refund for Cancelled Orders

  • Approved cancellations will be refunded within 5–7 business days.
  • Refunds will be issued via the original payment method.

4. Exceptions

  • Custom-built or pre-ordered products may not be eligible for cancellation.
  • Phonetech reserves the right to cancel orders in rare cases of stock issues or payment failures.

Still have questions? Contact our team at Phonetech.click@gmail.com — we’re happy to help!
